I'll try not to make these too long winded. Cory Henry is probably the best keyboard/organ player going around. He's just so good. Probably most famous is [Lingus](https://youtu.be/L_XJ_s5IsQc?si=2hRr9MBg7_TvD2wT), from snarky puppy. Also a quick shoutout to the drummer, Larnell Lewis, who also smashed it. 

Always hard to live up to a legend, but I'm a big fan of his cover of [purple rain](https://youtu.be/I8j7XJxew7s?si=14edt4qLgpGgv4kE).

His other solo stuff is really good. Quite gospel/funk rich. A pretty safe bet in my opinion, although I don't listen to him a lot these days.

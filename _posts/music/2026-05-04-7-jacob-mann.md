There's a bit of a running theme where a lot of my music comes from existing artists I listen to. Jacob Mann works a lot with Louis Cole, who features on his big band stuff.

[Don't fly spirit](https://youtu.be/uwP_ngIivks?si=9Y3BYLSN6MCEYZMF). I've linked to the album version, because it has the big chunk of silence. For me it makes the whole track. I also like how 'school brass band' it sounds, like it's been recorded in the school cafeteria. I like when things don't sound too polished.

This is probably one that most people won't be as into, it's probably more of a personal thing having spent a lot of time in brass bands not sounding nearly as good as this.

We were both pretty tired after yesterday, and I was dealing with a big of back pain. I'm aware of the fact that a 28 year old is having trouble keeping up with a 60 something year old. I don't find it amusing, no. Ok maybe a bit.

Decide to spend the day at home, not wanting to tire ourselves out too much. We did venture out locally, went to the local supermarket and got some bento susi and tempura, as well as some bread and eggs and milk, plus a few snacks and fruit. Everything was tasty.

We bought some strawberries which were a little expensive, but since we've arrived in Osaka they've been everywhere, especially in market stalls. My hot take is that they're not going to be anything special, we get good strawberries in England. Same with apples.

Tomorrow we'll go out again, plan is Osaka castle. Other than that not much to report from today, I was catching up on some youtube videos and caught the first quali of the new F1 season. Was interesting seeing the new cars beause they've brought in a bunch of rule changes, some of which seem a bit rubbish, some cool. Also fun because so many of the cars are just not ready yet, and lots of teams are having reliability issues. Verstappen crashed out in Q1 because his rear just locked up.

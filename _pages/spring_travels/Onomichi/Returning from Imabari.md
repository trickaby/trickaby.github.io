title: Returning from Imabari
---

The day after the cycle trip I was in good spirits, tired but in a good way. 

After breakfast of snacks I went to the bus station. Got on the bus to Fukuyama. Walked up to Fukuyama castle which is right outside the train station, as I had some time to kill until the next train. 

Got on the train a few stops to Onomichi.

Met up with mum who had stayed in Onomichi while I cycled, we went for ramen lunch, also had sardine tempura and chicken skin skewers. Local specialities. They were alright but probably haven't caught on for a reason. 

Walked around afterwards, mainly along the seafront. Weather was really nice again so we enjoyed it. Headed back home for a bit before going out for a pizza dinner. 

Look, I just really fancied a pizza ok. It was pretty good to be fair, classic Neapolitan style. They had some weird creations on the menu but I wasn't in the adventurous mood. Hiroshima next.

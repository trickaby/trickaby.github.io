Our next day in Ube was another relaxing one, as planned. We went to do some laundry at a laundrette about 2 minutes walk away, then went to the nearest supermarket for food, which was a 15 minute walk.

We've quite enjoyed walking around the supermarkets in Japan, a lot more so than walking down shopping streets.

On our way back we picked up our finished laundry and headed back to the room. The afternoon was spent indoors, it was sunny out but not super warm, but the sun came through our window and melted some of our chocolates on the windowsill. 

In the late afternoon, we headed down to the onsen and when we came up mum had bartered some green tea from downstairs, which we enjoyed in the evening, along with our supermarket haul.
